PREtirement Planning - Monte Carlo Projections

Your data stays with you. All computation is done on your browser and your data never leaves your device.

Fully configure your Monte Carlo simulations

  • Unlimited time-bound income sources

    Most retirement projections start from the retirement date. With PREtire, you can configure as many income sources as you want across different dates, so you can plan ahead whether you're just starting your career, considering a move to a slower-paced job, or ready to semi-retire before you give up work completely.

  • Plan for big purchases, sales, or other changes

    Planning to sell your house in a couple years, then rent for a year, then maybe buy a bigger house? Plug all your planned sales, purchases, and changes to monthly expenses into the Life Events settings tab and see the projection graphs adjust to your plans.

  • Fully customize your asset allocation

    Set up as many asset classes as you like, and make adjustments to the simulator even down to the standard deviation of each asset class.
